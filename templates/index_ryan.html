About Us & This Data Visualization

This dashboard was built to study the impact of Covid19 on unemployment in the US. The main motivation behind the study is the following: Historical development of confirmed Covid cases in the US. Which top 10 states have historically been impacted. Which states have been more impacted in terms of unemployment claims? How is the correlation between rise in cases and rise in unemployment claims varying overtime? This dashboard was built as part of a group project for Data Visualization and Analytics bootcamp in Minnesota. We are a group of Aspiring Data Scientists who come from various academic backgrounds and expertise, but are passionate about Coding and Data.

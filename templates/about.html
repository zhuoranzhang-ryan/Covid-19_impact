About This Data Visualization

This dashboard was built to study the impact of COVID-19 on unemployment in the US. The main motivation behind the study is the following:


  • Which top 10 states have historically been impacted.
  • Which states have been more impacted in terms of unemployment claims?
  • How is the correlation between rise in cases and rise in unemployment claims varying overtime?
  • Historical development of confirmed Covid cases in the US.

This dashboard was built as part of a group project for Data Visualization and Analytics bootcamp in Minnesota. We are a group of Aspiring Data Scientists who come from various academic backgrounds and expertise, but are passionate about Coding and Data.
